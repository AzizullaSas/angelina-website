About Angelina

Angelina is a Honolulu-based dance artist who weaves the grace of oriental dance with the dreamlike spirit of the sea. Every performance is crafted to feel luminous, feminine, and unforgettable.

From shimmering mermaid showcases to elegant solo sets, she performs across the Hawaiian Islands and beyond — bringing enchantment to weddings, resorts, and celebrations large and intimate.

  • StyleOriental & mermaid fusion
  • Available forEvents, galas & private celebrations
  • Based inHonolulu, O‘ahu (Hawaii)
